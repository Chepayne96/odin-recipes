Egg in a hole

Description

This simple yet great breakfast recipe is an absolute must for your morning breakfast cycle. Simple but a lil extra effort you can make this dish one the hang on the wall.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Large Egg
  • Butter
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preparing.Cut a circle out in the middle of your bread, put both to the side. Throw butter and oil into your skillet.
  2. Cook.Once the butter is melted and mixed with the oil add your bread, flip to get both sides covered in butter. Add the large egg in the middle of the bread. Cook till both sides of the egg are to your liking.
  3. ServeTop with what toppings you want, I like maple syrup added, and enjoy.
