Stirfry

Description

This is a nice easy to make, easy to clean up after stirfry. This dish is perfect for the nights you dont know what to make, or the days you need to meal prep. This dish has it all, chicken, vegies, and just the right amount of kick.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • Bell pepper
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Green onion & Sesame seeds
  • Chicken broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Rice vinegar
  • Arrowroot powder

Steps

  1. Make the stir-fry sauce.Whisk together the chicken broth, soy saucem sesame oil, dry sherry, honey, rice vinegar, and arrowroot powder in a small bowl.
  2. Saute the chicken.In a large skillet or wok on medium high heat, cook the chicken with salt, pepper, and a little avocado oil for about 4-5 minutes until lightly browned. Then remove to a plate.
  3. Cook the vegetables.Add a splash more oil, then saute the bell pepper, onion, carrot, and broccoli until tender.
  4. Stir-fry everything together!Add the chicken back in with garlic, ginger, and stir-fry sauce. Toss it all together until everything is well coated in the sauce.
