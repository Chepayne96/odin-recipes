Shrimp Spaghetti

Description

This spice twist on the classic spaghetti will leave you amazed. Slice some peppers and bake you some garlic bread and you will have a meal fit for a king or queen.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Spaghetti
  • Crushed tomatoes/li>
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Red Crushed Peppers
  • Cayenne Peppers
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh lemon
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.Follow the package instructions or make how you like.
  2. Cook the shrimp.Season the shrimp with Italian seasoning, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook for one minute on each side. Add the fresh lemon juice. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Making the sauce.Caramelize the onions and garlic. Stir in the red crushed pepper. Add the crushed tomatoes, sugar, fresh basil, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Combine.Place the shrimp back into the skillwt along with the spaghetti and cook for an additional minute. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve warm.
