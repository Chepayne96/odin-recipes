No Bake Cookies

Description

This classic no-bake cookie recipe is chewy, chocolatey, and simply irresistible â€” its the perfect quick and easy way to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Peanut butter
  • Oats
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Boil the sugar, butter, mild, and cocoa for 90 seconds.
  2. Remove from heat, then stir in the peanut butter.
  3. Stir in the oats and vanilla.
  4. Drop onto wax paper and let cool.
